Truffles Therapy creates safe, healing, and loving environments where our guests can experience the profound healing effects of magic truffles.

A beautiful retreat setting in nature, heart-centered professional tripsitters, proper diet, relaxation practices, yoga, meditation, hugs, integration circles, and a community of like-minded individuals are all part of the healing process.

Our Holistic Approach

At Truffles Therapy, you will enjoy a holistic approach to healing that incorporates every aspect of your being — mental, physical, emotional, spiritual. We allow you to relax and feel safe with a community of like-minded individuals, offer love-infused vegan cooking, and provide the space for you to connect with yourself, others, and with nature.

Preparation

From the time you submit your application to the moment of ingesting the truffles, we do our best to help you feel create the ideal mindset for your psychedelic journey. Yoga, meditation, and other wisdom practices are an integral part of the process. Well-intentioned space holders will be here to support you emotionally and mentally. By the time your journey starts, you will feel relaxed and comfortable.

Journey

During your magic truffles journey, we invite you to lie comfortably on a mattress with eyeshades on while listening to a playlist of soothing music. During the experience, we will be completely present with you but will not interfere with your journey unless asked to. We will remain nearby at all times to ensure you feel safe to surrender to your internal healing intelligence. We take care of all your physical needs such as food, shelter, warmth, comfort, and cosy blankets. If you need tissues, water, or help to go to the bathroom, we are here for you. When you are ready to share your experiences, feelings, and insights, we listen to you as long as you want to chat.

Integration

After your journey, we allow for at least one full day and night of rest to integrate the lessons learned and transformations experienced during the experience. Sharing circles, time in nature, one-on-one chats with spaceholders, and laughing with other participants are all part of the healing.

Our commitment to you

We are called to serve medicine in sacred ceremony with integrity, humility, and compassion. By creating a safe space and working only with experienced, well-intentioned healers and facilitators, we uphold the lessons of the medicines and build a foundation of mutual trust, authenticity, and respect. We are not gurus, preachers, or enlightened beings – our maestros are healers who humbly transmit the knowledge and healing energies of the plants, and our facilitators are devoted to helping guests face, process, and explore difficult experiences and emotions, handle projections, manage expectations, and surrender to the process. We strive to uphold the highest integrity in everything we do and be a beacon for responsible, safe, and ethical practices, such that our guests feel safe and supported to get the most out of their healing.
