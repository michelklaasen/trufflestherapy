What are truffles?

Psilocybin truffles are the underground part of psilocybin (magic) mushroom. Truffles and mushrooms contain the same active psychoactive ingredients, psilocybin and psilocin, which create profound psychedelic experiences.

Truffles are 100% legal in the Netherlands and are more predictable and consistent compared to mushrooms.
